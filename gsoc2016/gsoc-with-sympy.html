GSoC 2016 Phase I : Github, Patch, PR and Proposal

My proposal on Group Theory with SymPy has been accepted.


I have been accepted for GSoC2016 project with SymPy to work on Group Theory. First, a little bit about SymPy, a computer algebra system written entirely in Python. SymPy 1.0 was released one month ago. I will be working on Group Theory over the summer, for the next 3 months, to implement CGT and Group Theory, which are parts of mathematics I particularly enjoy. You can view my project proposal GSoC 2016 Application Gaurav Dhingra: Group Theory. Until a few days ago I was pretty busy with my exams, but in the next few weeks i will go over working on the project. I will particularly focus on `Finite and Finitely Presented Groups`.
